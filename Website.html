About me

I'm a journalist and master's student at Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism. I grew up in Oberlin, Ohio and attended Oberlin College, graduating in 2023. I have multiple professional experiences including work for iHeartMedia in Cleveland and The Morning Journal, a daily newspaper in Lorain, Ohio. Sports reporting and feature writing are my areas of speciality within journalism.
